The Zest marketing community plugin enables blog owners, content writers, for content creators to contribute content easily from WordPress.

== Description ==

Zest adds a listener to your post editor and every time a post becomes public, the plugin contributes the link on behalf of the logged-in community member.

The Zest Marketing Community plugin requires an active membership on Zest. For more information, please visit the [Zest's Marketing Community Page](https://zest.is/marketing).

== Installation ==

Upload the Zest Marketing Community plugin to your blog, activate it, and then sign in with your account.

1, 2, 3: You're done!

[Watch the YouTube video for guided installation](https://youtu.be/suF4GmpRiBU)
